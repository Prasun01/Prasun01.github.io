About

Structural Geometry
& Organic Chaos.

Prasun Mishra is a visual artist based in India, specializing in the juxtaposition of brutalist architectural geometry and the fluid chaos of natural landscapes.

His photography isolates the silent, overlooked details of the world—the sharp edge of a building against the sky, or the soft texture of mist in a forest. By stripping away the visual noise, he reveals a minimalist narrative where architecture and nature exist in a delicate, high-contrast balance.

His style is defined by clean lines, negative space, and a desaturated palette that emphasizes texture over color.
